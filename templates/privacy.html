1. Information We Collect

VidGrab is committed to protecting your privacy. We do NOT collect or store:

2. How We Process Videos

Our service works by:

  • Processing video URLs directly through yt-dlp
  • Storing temporary files only during active downloads

We use yt-dlp, an open-source video downloader, to process your requests. No data is shared with third-party advertising or analytics services.

4. Data Security

All processing happens in temporary memory and files are immediately deleted after download. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
